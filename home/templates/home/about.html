About Us

Hash Logic is an Information Technology Consultancy & Training Company in Chandigarh. Hash Logic was started to meet the skill requirements of the industry and generate more employment especially in tier-2, tier-3 towns and in the rural part of the country. While the training of teachers and students would be the primary objective, the Hash Logic will also provide various services related to information & technology both for public and private sector. Hash Logic will be working through a four pillar program in the area of, Student Skill Development, Industry-Institute Interaction and Research & Development and Corporate training. One of the prospectuses of hash logic is to provide a holistic service to every stakeholder of the education ecosystem in developing the next generation of talent pool in India to make them industry ready employees, innovators, entrepreneurs and leaders. To achieve this. the training programs and the learning course curriculum have been meticulously designed with the collaborative efforts of the academicians and industry professionals which will build highly skilled working professionals hence contributing towards the skilled India Mission started by Government of India.

Mrs. Shruti Wadhwa (Director)

Shruti Wadhwa received B.Tech. from PTU, M.Tech. from the National Institute of Technical Teacher’s
Training & Research (NITTTR), Chandigarh.She is the Chief Technical Officer of Hash Logic Pvt. Ltd.
Her areas of research interest include Computer Networks, IOT, Data Science, Machine Learning, Web
Security,Cyber Security, and Cloud Computing, Virtualization & Artificial Intelligence. To her credit,
she has many research publications in referred to International and National Journals and
Conferences. She also delivered expert lectures on Web Security Audit at Sardar Patel University
of Police Security and Criminal Justice, Jodhpur and in different states of India.She has delivered
many expert lectures in National Institute of Technical Teacher’s Training & Research (NITTTR),
Chandigarh and in Panjab University. She has more than 7 years of experience in teaching and
organizing various training programs in the areas of CSE and IT for the faculty of engineering
colleges, polytechnics, and students.She is active in research areas and has done many projects and
many students are pursuing thesis under her guidance.



The other aspect of the company is to provide innovative solutions to the various IT related problems. The services provided by the company are Web development, Web designing, Application Development, Security Solutions and Research & Development projects.
